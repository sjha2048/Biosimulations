1. Technology arrow_upward

BioSimulations is implemented using the following technologies:

  • Models: CellML , SBML
  • Simulation experiments: SED-ML
  • Simulations: COPASI , VCell
  • Data visualization: Vega , Vega-lite
  • Web framework: Angular
  • Authentication: Auth0
  • HTML components: Material Design
  • Theme: Material Theme Generator
  • Icons: Font Awesome , Material Design
  • Source code version control: GitHub
  • Containers: Docker
  • Continuous integration: GitHub

2. Development team arrow_upward

BioSimulations was developed by the Karr Lab at the Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai in New York and the Center for Cell Analysis and Modeling at the University of Connecticut Health Center as part of the Center for Reproducible Biomodeling Modeling .
